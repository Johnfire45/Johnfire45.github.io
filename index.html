Engagement

How It Works

A straightforward engagement process with clear milestones at each stage.

01

Discovery Call

We discuss your product, scope, and security concerns. I listen before I quote — pricing follows scope, not the other way around.

02

Scope & Proposal

You complete a short questionnaire. I send a proposal with deliverables, timeline, and fixed pricing within 2–3 business days.

03

Kick-off & Testing

After agreement and advance payment, testing begins. Critical findings are flagged immediately — not held until the final report.

04

Report & Remediation Call

Draft report delivered for review, followed by a call to walk through findings and discuss fixes with your team.

05

Final Report & Retesting

Final report delivered after payment clearance. One formal retest round included to verify fixes are in place.
